  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp oil

  3. Mix dal and gongura together.
  4. Heat oil, add mustard, cumin, red chilies, garlic and onion.
  5. Pour this into dal mixture. Mix and serve hot!

Ingredients:

  • 2 raw mangoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mustard powder
  • 1/4 cup red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp fenugreek seeds

Steps:

  1. Dry mango pieces fully.
  2. Roast fenugreek, grind to powder.
  3. Mix mustard, chili powder, salt, turmeric.
  4. Add mango, fenugreek, and oil. Mix well.
  5. Store in a jar. Ready in 3 days!
